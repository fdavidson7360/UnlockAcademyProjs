I chose to study Computer Science in order to integrate my mathematics skills to help minority students. This drive comes from growing up in underprivileged communities where resources were scarce or nonexistent in regards to the STEM fields. Personally, I believe seeing someone that looks like me providing me with tools necessary to be successful provides me with a sense of I can do it too. Although it was not until I reached my later years of high school that I received this type of support, it still made a major impact on the changes I needed to make in order to be succesful.
