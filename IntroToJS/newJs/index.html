Impetus

My ultimate goal in life is to embody the definition of service. Service is more than just random acts of kindness or picking up trash from the side of the rode. True service requires diligence, hard work and placing the needs of others above yourself. I feel that if we can all recycle those principles it will inevitably build a strong and resilent community. For many years, I thought that focusing on a singular task as an indiviual with complete focus was the path to success. In reality success is a conglomeration of failures, favorable outcomes, lessons leraned and triumphs with accountabilty parteners. This creative balance is what allows us to gain perspective in any situation. The way I can personally contribute to this is by bringing a small portion of other people's dreams to life.
